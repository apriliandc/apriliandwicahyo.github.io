The idea of ​​making the Digibook is expected that the concept will increase the interest of everyone, especially young people, in reading. My goal was to develop this new platform to be more user-friendly, fun, and interactive and increase interest in reading with the Digibook platform.

BACKGROUND

Based on a survey conducted by the Program for International Student Assessment (PISA) released by the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) in 2021, Indonesia ranks 62 out of 70 countries, or is the bottom 10 countries with low literacy levels. From the survey, Indonesian people especially young people can be categorized as lazy reading. thus i ask myself:

“ HOW MIGHT WE MAKE READING ACTIVITIES MORE INTERACTIVE AND FUN”
